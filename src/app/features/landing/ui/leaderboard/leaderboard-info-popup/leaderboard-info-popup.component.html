ℹ️ How It Works This leaderboard shows the top topics based on pulses (support taps) during the selected month.
  • You can pulse a topic once every 24 hours.
  • Topics are ranked by the total number of pulses received within the month.
  • In case of a tie, the topic with the most recent pulse ranks higher.
  • Use the dropdown to explore rankings from previous months.
Note: Consistent daily support makes a difference. A topic backed by a few passionate supporters pulsing every day can outrank one with many who only pulse once. The leaderboard reflects both reach and sustained commitment.
