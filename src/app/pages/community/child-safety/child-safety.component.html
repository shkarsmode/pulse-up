Pulse Up – Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: March 30, 2025

At Pulse Up, we are committed to protecting our users and preventing the sharing of content that harms or exploits minors. We take child safety seriously and comply with all applicable laws and Google Play policies related to child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Pulse Up allows users to report inappropriate content or topics, including anything that may compromise the safety of children. Users can report content directly within the app by tapping the “Report” option on any topic.

If you believe a topic, image, or user may pose a child safety concern or involves CSAM, you can also contact our team directly at: Email: safety@pulseup.com

We promptly investigate all reports and remove content that violates our policies.

Prevention Measures

  • All topics and content submitted by users are reviewed for safety and compliance.
  • We do not permit content that exploits, endangers, or targets children inappropriately.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement where required.

Legal Compliance

Pulse Up complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations and works with national and international agencies when necessary.
