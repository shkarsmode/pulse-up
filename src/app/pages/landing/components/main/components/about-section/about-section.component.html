A New Way to Decide What Matters

We believe that what truly matters should be decided by people — not algorithms, corporations, or gatekeepers. Pulse Up lets anyone show support for a cause, trend, or event — with complete anonymity. Supporting what matters shouldn’t feel like a burden — it should be effortless, exciting, and empowering. That’s why we designed Pulse Up to be quick, engaging, and rewarding.
